About me

Senior Consultant
@ innoQ Deutschland GmbH

I have many years of experience building and maintaining software for the JVM. Currently, my main interests are Microservice- and Cloud-Architectures, DevOps, the Spring-Framework and Clojure.

As a senior consultant for software architecture and engineering at INNOQ, I help clients by building well crafted and value providing software.

I am also the writer of a column in the german magazine JavaSPEKTRUM where I publish articles about JVM, infrastructure, and architectural topics every two months.
